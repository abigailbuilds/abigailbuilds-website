Usage Guidelines

Deep Teal (#0F5F5C): Primary brand color for headers, buttons, CTAs, and key navigation elements.

Tech Mint (#b3e2da): Backgrounds, subtle accents, and section dividers. Creates breathing room.

Apricot Pro (#d97c32): Accent color for highlights, hover states, important labels, and visual interest.

Off-White (#FAF9F6): Main backgrounds and light surfaces. More elegant than pure white.
